Quest List

Main Quests HAVE to be completed in order, there is a level that quests unlock at but you still will not be able to access that quest if you are at the correct level but haven’t completed a main quest before it.

Main Quests:

  • Tutorial
  • Bread Winner- unlocks the cooking hobby (level 6)
  • Money Grows On Trees- unlocks the simoleon sprout and life point lotus plants (level 7)
  • Love Is In The Air- unlocks the ability to marry your sims (level 7)
  • Two and a Half Sims– unlocks babies (level 8)
  • Ocean View– unlocks the beach side premium houses (level 9)
  • The Mysterious Island– unlocks the island and monunments to get bonuses on actions(level 10)
  • Raiders of the Lost Artifacts– find materials to build the monuments
  • Need for Steed– unlocks stable and horses (level 11)*
  • A Quest for Toddlers– unlocks toddlers (level 12)
  • Going Swimmingly– unlocks the swimming center and garden pools (level 13)
  • Hidden Unicorn– unlocks the show jumping hobby (level 14)*
  • In Da Clubhouse– unlocks a toddler hobby (level 14)*
  • Preparing for Preteens– unlocks preteens (level 15)
  • Sous Judgemental– unlocks the restaurant (level 16)*
  • An Alien Concept: Weather Machine– unlocks the weather machine, snow park and winter clothing (level 16)
  • Multi Story Renovations– unlocks second and third floors (level 17)
  • Teenagers– unlocks teens (level 18)
  • Higher Education– unlocks education for teens (level 19)
  • The Pirate and His Goddess– unlocks pirate ship for pirate ship trading (level 19)*
  • The Road to Fame– unlocks teens musical hobby (level 20)
  • Adulthood– unlocks the ability to age teens to adults (level 21)
  • Ghost Hunters– unlocks the ghost hunting hobby and haunted items (level 22)
  • Seniors– unlocks seniors (level 23)
  • Bird Feeding– unlocks seniors hobby (level 24)
  • In Stitches– unlocks seniors hobby (level 25)
  • Life Dreams and Legacies– unlocks death and automatic ageing (level 26)
